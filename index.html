TinkHerHack

Tink-her-Hack is an all-Kerala women’s exclusive hackathon organized by TinkerHub Women in Tech. Our mission is to bring together women from various campuses across Kerala, providing them with a platform to learn, develop, and be inspired. Whether you’re a beginner eager to dive into tech or a seasoned professional looking to enhance your skills, Tink-her-Hack is the place to be.

Objectives

  • Foster a supportive community of women in technology.
  • Build a solid core knowledge of tech stacks.
  • Upskill abilities for a stronger portfolio.

Venues

This year, we are excited to host 1000 participants from across Kerala, spread across various colleges. Our impact extends beyond the hackathon, empowering women to pursue tech careers and contribute to the industry.

Overview

Prepare for an exhilarating experience at TinkerHack 2024:

  • Minimum 2-4 participants per team

  • Each group will be assigned a dedicated mentor
